Gumshoe

/ˈɡʌmʃuː/ meaning - a detective.

Coming soon. Gumshoe is the application for tracking you content with single url. The single url can be used for shortening the url, open tracking (emails open or not), click tracking (link clicked or not) and to check the visitors of anypage.

The project is still under development. Contact Manish Sahani for more info.
